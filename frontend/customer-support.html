Pricing & Payments

How are fuel prices calculated? expand_more
Our fuel prices are synced in real-time with local market rates. We add a small service fee to cover logistics and delivery to your doorstep. You can always see the final price breakdown before confirming your order in the app.
Is there a minimum delivery fee? expand_more
No, there is no minimum amount required for fueling, however, a standard delivery fee of $4.99 applies to all orders under 10 gallons.
local_shipping

Delivery Times

How long does a refill take? expand_more
Most deliveries are completed within 30 to 45 minutes of booking. On-demand fueling times may vary during peak hours or extreme weather conditions.
Can I schedule a delivery in advance? expand_more
Yes! You can schedule a delivery up to 7 days in advance. Simply select the "Schedule" option during checkout and pick your preferred time slot.
health_and_safety

Safety Protocols

What are your on-site safety procedures? expand_more
Our riders follow strict Hazmat-certified procedures, including area cordoning, static discharge prevention, and spill containment protocols. All equipment is inspected daily.
Are riders certified to handle fuel? expand_more
Absolutely. Every Flanorx rider undergoes extensive training and holds required local certifications for hazardous material handling and fire safety.
