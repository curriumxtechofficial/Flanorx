Deliveries & Routes

How are deliveries assigned to riders? expand_more
Deliveries are assigned based on your proximity to the pickup location, your current availability, and rider rating. The system prioritizes the nearest available rider to ensure the fastest service for customers.
Can I choose which deliveries to accept? expand_more
Yes, you have the freedom to accept or decline any delivery request. However, your acceptance rate may affect future assignment priority. We recommend only declining when absolutely necessary.
What if I can't find the delivery location? expand_more
Use the in-app chat feature to contact the customer directly. You can also call through our masked number system for privacy. If still unable to locate, contact rider support for assistance.
payments

Earnings & Payouts

When will I receive my earnings? expand_more
Earnings are processed every Monday for the previous week's deliveries. We also offer instant cash-out 24/7 for a small fee - funds typically arrive within 2-4 hours to your linked account.
How is my delivery payout calculated? expand_more
Your payout = Base delivery fee + distance multiplier + surge pricing (during peak hours) - platform fee. You can view a complete breakdown in the Earnings tab after each delivery.
What are peak hours for bonus pay? expand_more
Peak hours are typically 7-9 AM and 5-7 PM on weekdays, with weekend lunch hours also seeing increased demand. During these times, surge pricing can multiply your earnings by 1.5x or more.
health_and_safety

Safety & Compliance

What safety equipment is required? expand_more
All riders must wear provided PPE including flame-retardant gloves, high-visibility safety vest, and closed-toe shoes. Your delivery kit includes spill mats, fire extinguisher, and emergency contact cards.
What to do in case of a fuel spill? expand_more
Follow your spill protocol training: secure the area immediately, deploy spill kit materials, document the incident in the app, and contact emergency support. The app has a dedicated "Spill Report" button for quick reporting.
How to keep my certifications current? expand_more
Check your certification status in Profile → Certifications. We send reminders 30 days before expiration. You can schedule recertification courses directly through the app at discounted rates.
